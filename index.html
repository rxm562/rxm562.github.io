I am a PhD Candidate in the Department of Civil Engineering at Case Western Reserve University. My advisor is Dr. Yue Li. My PhD research includes developing physically-based pipeline failure prediction model, modeling the functional interdependency between water and road infrastructure systems, and developing risk-informed decision-making tools to support asset management.

Before I joined Case, I graduated with an M.Eng. in Earthquake Engineering from University of Rome 'La Sapienza'. My Master's Dissertation was focused on Non-Linear Performance Evaluation of Masonry Walls.
